Emergency Care Disclaimer

Section 09

Luma Dental Care Clinic provides dental emergency services during our operating hours. Our emergency dental care covers conditions such as severe toothache, dental trauma, lost fillings or crowns, dental abscesses, and post-surgical complications.

Luma Dental Care is a dental clinic and is not equipped to handle life-threatening medical emergencies. In the event of a systemic medical emergency (cardiac arrest, anaphylaxis, severe allergic reaction, etc.), please call 108 (Ambulance) or proceed to the nearest hospital emergency immediately.
  • Dental emergencies outside clinic hours may be directed to our emergency helpline, which is attended on a best-effort basis.
  • Emergency consultations carry a separate fee, which will be communicated before treatment.
  • Luma Dental Care is not liable for any adverse outcomes arising from delays in seeking emergency care at an appropriate medical facility.
